Frequently Asked Questions

What types of products can I purchase on this website?

You can purchase a wide range of traditional Indian products, including Puja Samagri, Handicrafts, Traditional Attires, regional sweets, and other culturally significant items made by local artisans from different parts of India.

How do I know the products are of high quality

Our platform ensures quality by requiring all sellers to undergo a thorough registration and verification process. Additionally, we use customer reviews and ratings to maintain high standards for all products listed on our site.

Can I shop on this website if I live outside India?

Yes, our website is designed to cater to the global Indian diaspora. We offer international shipping to over 200 countries, so you can enjoy authentic Indian products no matter where you are.

How is shipping handled, and what are the costs?

We partner with reliable postal and courier services to offer standard and affordable shipping rates. Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your location and the size of your order.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse the products, add your desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in if you already have one. After entering your shipping and payment details, you'll receive a confirmation email with your order details.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen. Estimated delivery times will be provided at checkout. International orders typically take longer to arrive, but we strive to deliver as quickly as possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and international payment gateways, ensuring a secure and convenient shopping experience.

What if I receive a damaged or incorrect product?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our customer support team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will assist you in resolving the issue through a replacement, refund, or return.

How do you ensure that the products are authentic?

We work directly with verified local artisans and sellers, ensuring that all products are authentic and reflect the rich cultural heritage of India.

How can I contact customer support if I have questions or issues?

You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website, or by emailing us directly at support@gmail.com. We're here to help with any questions or concerns you may have.
